MAC Address

Media Access Control
    - Permanent
    - Physical
    - Unique

Assigned by manufacturer

Unique MAC address on a network device

Changing MAC address:
    - Makes you anonymous
    - Impersonate other devices
    - Bypass filters

How to change MAC address manually:

--> commands:
ifconfig
ifconfig eth0 down
ifconfig eth0 hw ether <new ether address>
ifconfig eth0 up

eth0 can be swithced with whatever network device u want (ex: eth0, wlan0 etc)
